# Insights

## Insight 1

Greed market sentiment produced the highest average profit per trade (Average PnL ≈ 87.89), indicating stronger profitability compared to Fear and Neutral market conditions.

---

## Insight 2

Extreme Greed recorded the highest trader win rate (≈49%), while Neutral sentiment produced the lowest win rate (≈31.7%).

---

## Insight 3

Fear periods contained the highest number of executed trades, suggesting significantly higher trading activity during fearful market conditions.

---

## Insight 4

Buy and Sell trades remained relatively balanced across market sentiments, although Sell trades slightly outnumbered Buy trades in most categories.
